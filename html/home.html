Rent a Ride, available for FREE download on iOS, Android, and Windows Phone platforms, is the largest private car app in Philippines that enables passengers
to book cars in just a few taps of their smartphones. We aim to revamp the local car service industry by introducing simple, cost effective
mobile-based technology to both the supply (drivers) and demand (passenger) sides of the distribution chain. With this technology,
Rent a Ride hopes to optimize the matching process between drivers and passengers.
